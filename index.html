Features

If you want to read all about our project, you can read our whitepaper.

Private Send & Receive

Shade Addresses and Ring Signatures. Stealth addresses are a convenient method of protecting the privacy of data and ioc receivers. It works by getting the payment sender to generate a one time address based on a single public Stealth address.

Encrypted messaging

AES 256 Encrypted Fully Decentralized Messaging Platform. It permits build a censorship resistant decentralized “Twitter-esque” or “Whatsapp-like" service. Your third party application can also use the messaging system!

De-central data storage on the Blockchain

With each DION alias there exists the option to upload a data value, it is base64 encoded and the underlying data may be (for example) of the form ASCII, PDF, JPEG or any binary data.

Decentralized DNS

DIONS are aliases and provide a human readable name, which can be resolved directly from the blockchain. Alias can uniquely ascribe to an ordinary I/O COIN address.

Application Based Blockchain

Proof of Stake. 60 Second Blocks. 4MB Blocks. Private, Channel & Atomic Keys, BIP65, Removed Coinage. HTML5 Wallet. API for Decentralized based applications.

More to come

I/O SuperChain, codename "Chameleon". Ballots for secret voting. Graduated staking. Scientific computation. Decentralized I/O Exchange, codename DIOEX.

History

Time has a wonderful way of showing us what really matters.

  • July 23, 2014

    Genesis Block

    Looking to improve Bitcoin with a more energy efficient cryptocurrency, Scott Nadal and Sunny King released a whitepaper for a proof of stake blockchain currency named Peercoin. Inspired by Sunny King, After years of POS proof of concept testing and blockchain penetration testing, the I/O Digital dev team lead by it's founder Joel Bosh, devised a unique approach to POS and the first I/O Coin Genesis block was mined on July 23, 2014.

  • Agust, 2014

    Fair Launch

    Refusing to ICO to facilitate adoption, the development team opted for a Fair Launch. I/O Coin was launched via X11 POW mining and after 14 days, the blockchain was successfully transitioned to its unique POS.

  • 2017

    Fully tested

    After 3 years of hard testing and 100% uptime, the development team was ready to tests its boundaries and pushed for a major upgrade.

  • 2017

    DIONS

    Decentralized I/O Name Server is the first application to make use of the enhanced I/O blockchain, allowing users to register simple, human-readable and easily-memorized aliases to send a receive I/O Coin payments with.

  • 2017

    Fully encrypted messaging

    Decentralized messaging system which enable fast, easy and secure communication between all I/O Digital users.
